Picking a phone-system vendor is mostly about trust. 3CX uses a public partner tier system — Bronze, Silver, Gold, Titanium, Platinum — so you can actually verify how deep a provider's experience is before you sign anything.

## What "Platinum" certifies

Platinum is awarded to partners who consistently deliver:

- **High deployment volume** across multiple industries and country jurisdictions.
- **Active certified engineers** (not just resellers — people who can architect SBC, SIP, and call-routing topologies).
- **Long-term renewals**, which means clients keep their systems instead of churning out.

It is not a marketing badge — it requires re-qualification, and 3CX lists every partner publicly so it can be checked.

## What changes for you as a customer

1. **Faster, deeper support.** Platinum partners escalate directly into 3CX engineering when something rare happens at 2 a.m.
2. **Better pricing on annual licenses.** Platinum tier unlocks volume pricing that smaller resellers cannot match.
3. **Migration safety.** Moving from an old PBX (Avaya, Mitel, NEC, hosted Vonage) is where most projects fail. Platinum partners have done the migration dozens of times.
4. **AI-readiness.** 3CX V20+ exposes APIs that let modern AI agents plug in cleanly. Platinum partners are already shipping that work.
