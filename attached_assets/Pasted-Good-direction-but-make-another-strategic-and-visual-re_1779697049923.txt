The site should feel more premium, more animated, and more visually unified. Right now the structure is better, but the design still needs polish.

Design direction:

Make the page feel like a serious communications and infrastructure company entering the AI era — not a template website and not an AI startup landing page.

Use a consistent visual language across all sections:
blue/navy/blue-gray as the main identity,
orange as the DSX Edge accent,
white/off-white only as breathing space,
cyan only for subtle signal/network details.

Do not let every section look like a different component library. The cards, gradients, borders, icons, spacing, and animations should feel like one system.

Animation direction:

Add subtle professional motion, not gimmicks.

* hero platform cards gently fade/slide in with staggered timing
* soft animated signal lines connecting the three layers
* slight glow/pulse on orange DSX AI Enabled accents
* cards lift subtly on hover
* section headers fade up as they enter viewport
* metrics count up smoothly
* CTA buttons have restrained hover movement
* background blobs move very subtly or remain static if motion feels cheap
* navbar transition should remain smooth and premium

Avoid:

* bouncy animations
* excessive neon
* spinning icons
* overused AI sparkle effects
* too much cyan
* motion that makes the page feel like a SaaS toy
